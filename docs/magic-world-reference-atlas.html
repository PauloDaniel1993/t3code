Design Compass

Use these as the three forces behind every region. The same location can have a living surface settlement, an ancient layer below it, and a corruptive occupation spreading through it.

Magic as Relationship

Villages, temples, guilds, festivals, music, farming, weather rites, and spirit bargains.

  • Materials: timber, stone, cloth, clay, copper, glass, living plants.
  • Shapes: terraces, courtyards, canals, tree-roots, shrines, wind towers.
  • Conflicts: broken pacts, sick forests, drought, forgotten local spirits.

Magic as System

Dungeons as spell infrastructure: crystal logic, song archives, artificial weather, and impossible machines.

  • Materials: white stone, crystal, ceramic, gold inlay, glass conduits.
  • Shapes: rings, lattices, vertical shafts, observatories, recursive rooms.
  • Conflicts: sealed weapons, failed utopias, living systems without masters.

Magic as Control

Enemy domains turn magic into extraction: anti-shrines, mana factories, rune engines, and suppressed nature.

  • Materials: black iron, brass, pipes, leather, pressure glass, artificial crystal.
  • Shapes: smokestacks, cages, turbines, gauges, cables, repeating military blocks.
  • Conflicts: polluted leylines, captured elementals, silenced forests.

Environment Prompts

Use these pairings when sketching maps. Each row gives you a normal-world expression, a dungeon expression, and a corruptive expression of the same region.

Forest Region

Living village: tree platforms, resin lamps, herb guilds. Ancient dungeon: bio-lab arboretum with extinct plants. Enemy version: iron braces choking trunks, sap pipes, spirit cages.

Ta Prohm Sagrada Familia industrial heritage

Mountain Region

Living village: cliff monasteries, wind bells, defensive stone chants. Ancient dungeon: weather-control observatory. Enemy version: mine-fortress, pressure towers, captured storm spirit.

Meteora Zeal Dishonored

Coastal Region

Living town: tidal shrine, shell instruments, moonlit ferries. Ancient dungeon: drowned transit hub and tide engine. Enemy version: polluted harbor with bottled water spirits powering cranes.

Mont Saint-Michel Petra hydraulics FFVII reactors

Capital Region

Living city: guild courts, music academies, warded bridges. Ancient dungeon: crystal civic archive below the palace. Enemy version: Gothic ministry district, propaganda choirs, rune-lit alleys.

Alhambra Book of the New Sun Arcane

Practical method: pick one real architectural anchor, one music anchor, and one story anchor per region. Example: "Meteora + Bulgarian choir + Chrono Trigger's Zeal" quickly suggests a mountain spirit academy built above a buried weather engine.
